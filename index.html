Italian Sassarese Pronunciation
Page 1

Based on the TOGO Dictionary, originally hosted at togo.sassari.tv.

K and Tz are removed in this version, to comply with Turritan Language Orthografic Standard.
There is some manual cleaning needed because while removing 'tz' cluster, many words that should be written with just a 'z' now got a double 'zz'.
